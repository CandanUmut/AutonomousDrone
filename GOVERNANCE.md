# Governance

## Roles
- **Maintainers:** Overall project stewardship and releases.
- **HW Lead:** Owns hardware requirements, BOM, airframe, propulsion, and safety hardware.
- **SW Lead:** Owns autonomy stack, simulation, telemetry, and safety software.

## Decision-making
- Consensus is preferred.
- If needed, Maintainers decide with input from HW Lead and SW Lead.

## Release cadence
- v1 roadmap milestones are documented in **roadmap/**.
- Quarterly minor releases, with ad-hoc patch releases for safety or compliance issues.
