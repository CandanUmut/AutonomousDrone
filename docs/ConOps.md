# Concept of Operations (ConOps)

## v1 scope
- Launch from a controlled site.
- Autonomous mission with waypoint navigation.
- Return and land in designated recovery zone.

## Stakeholders
- Operators (pilot-in-command)
- Safety officer
- Test crew

## Typical mission flow
1. Preflight check and mission planning.
2. Launch and climb to safe altitude.
3. Execute waypoint mission.
4. Return-to-home and recovery.
5. Postflight inspection and log review.
